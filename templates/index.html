About

Some information

What you can expect from Syn?

An active guild

There is plenty of activity going on. From progress raids to M+ to fun alt raids.

An inclusive guild

If you get stuck in here, you will have lots of fun with a bunch of like-minded people

Active officers

A calm and level headed team of officers with over a decade of combined experience.

Great raiding

Competitive, fun and a strong ethos of teamwork.

What we expect from you?

Attendance

At least 90-100% attendance is expected.

Preparation

To be fully prepared, tactic wise, for the progress bosses of that particular week.

Ambition

From raiders to socials, we want people who are naturally ambitious.

Competitiveness

We want people who always want to improve and play their class to their maximum potential.
